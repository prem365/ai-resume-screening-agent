Job Title: Junior AI Research Associate
Company: Rooman Technologies
Location: Remote / Bangalore, India

About the Role:
We are looking for a Junior AI Research Associate to help us design, build, and test next-generation AI agents. You will work closely with our senior research team to implement RAG pipelines, evaluate LLM performance, and build prototypes for various industry use cases.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design and implement AI agents using Python and popular LLM frameworks.
- Experiment with different prompting techniques (Chain-of-Thought, Few-Shot, etc.).
- Build and maintain RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) systems using vector databases.
- Clean and preprocess messy datasets for model evaluation.
- Document research findings and technical implementations.

Required Skills:
- Proficiency in Python programming.
- Understanding of LLMs (GPT, Claude, etc.) and their APIs.
- Experience with NLP libraries like spaCy, NLTK, or Transformers.
- Familiarity with vector databases (Pinecone, Chroma, etc.) is a plus.
- Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work independently.
- Excellent technical writing and documentation skills.

Education:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, AI, Data Science, or a related field.
